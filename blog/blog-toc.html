1. How i reuse code

Reusing code is among the more timesaving ways to manage a large website, when you want elements to reaper on all your different pages you might experience that you suddenly forget to apply a update to one of your sub-pages, suddenly your footer looks different on your blog compared to the landing-page, the best workaround for this is to write code just once. Then you can reuse that same code over and over
